## 中文版

### 一、我们收集的信息

**1. 财务记录数据（仅存储在您的设备本地）**

本应用在您的设备本地存储财务数据，包括：

- 交易记录（日期、金额、分类、描述、备注）
- 账户信息（名称、余额、类型）
- 分类与标签
- 交易照片/凭证附件
- 图表和统计数据

所有财务数据均经过 **AES-256-CBC** 加密后存储在您的设备上，加密密钥由操作系统安全存储（iOS/macOS: Keychain；Android: EncryptedSharedPreferences）。

**2. 生物识别认证（可选）**

如您启用生物识别解锁（Face ID、Touch ID 或指纹识别），相关认证数据由您的操作系统管理，我们无法访问。本应用仅在本地检验认证结果。

**3. CSV 数据导入 / 导出**

您可将财务数据导出为加密 CSV 文件。导出的文件完全由您控制，本应用不会将导出数据上传至任何地方。您也可导入之前备份的加密数据文件。

**4. 本地通知提醒（可选）**

如您开启账户到期提醒、预算警告或定期账单提醒，本应用将在您的设备上注册本地通知。仅存储您选择的提醒时间与偏好，不上传至任何服务器。通知由设备本地触发，与任何远程服务无关。

**5. 广告相关数据**

本应用使用 Google AdMob 展示广告。AdMob 可能收集：

- 广告标识符（IDFA），仅在您通过 App Tracking Transparency 授权后
- 设备类型、系统版本等非个人标识信息

您可随时在"设置 → 隐私与安全性 → 跟踪"中修改授权。

**6. 应用偏好设置**

语言选择、货币单位、主题设置、通知开关等偏好仅保存在本地，不上传至任何服务器。

## English Version

### 1. Information We Collect

**a) Financial Records (stored locally on your device only)**

Your financial data is stored locally on your device, including:

- Transaction records (date, amount, category, description, notes)
- Account information (name, balance, type)
- Categories and tags
- Transaction photos and receipt attachments
- Charts and analytics data

All financial data is encrypted with **AES-256-CBC** before being stored on your device. Encryption keys are managed by the operating system's secure storage (iOS/macOS: Keychain; Android: EncryptedSharedPreferences).

**b) Biometric Authentication (Optional)**

If you enable biometric unlock (Face ID, Touch ID, or fingerprint), authentication data is managed entirely by your operating system and we cannot access it. This app only verifies the authentication result locally.

**c) CSV Data Import / Export**

You may export your financial data as an encrypted CSV file. Exported files are entirely under your control — the app never uploads exported data anywhere. You may also import previously backed-up encrypted data files.

**d) Local Notification Reminders (Optional)**

If you enable account expiration alerts, budget warnings, or recurring bill reminders, the app registers local notifications on your device. Only your chosen reminder times and preferences are stored locally. Notifications are triggered entirely on-device and are not connected to any remote service.

**e) Advertising Data**

This app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

- Advertising Identifier (IDFA), only after you grant permission via App Tracking Transparency
- Non-personal device signals such as device type and OS version

You may change this permission at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

**f) App Preferences**

Your language, currency, theme, and notification preferences are saved locally only and are never uploaded to any server.
