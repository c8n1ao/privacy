## 中文版

### 一、我们收集的信息

**1. 训练数据（仅存储在您的设备本地）**

本应用在您的设备本地存储训练记录，包括：

- 测试模式（颜色文字 / 动物 / 植物）
- 会话开始与结束时间、总时长
- 每次试次的刺激内容、显示颜色、用户响应及反应时（毫秒）
- 认知分析结果（反应时均值/中位数/标准差、Stroop 干扰效应 SI、促进效应、Delta Score、IES、BIS、PES 等）

所有训练数据均经过 **AES-256-CBC** 加密后存储在您的设备上，加密密钥由操作系统安全存储（iOS/macOS: Keychain；Android: EncryptedSharedPreferences）。

**2. iCloud 同步（可选，仅限 iOS / macOS）**

如您的设备已登录 iCloud，训练记录将通过 Apple CloudKit 同步至您的个人 iCloud 账户。此数据仅属于您，我们无权访问。

**3. 训练提醒通知（可选）**

如您开启每日训练提醒，本应用将在您的设备上注册本地通知。仅存储您选择的提醒时间与星期偏好，不上传至任何服务器。通知由设备本地触发，与任何远程服务无关。

**4. 数据导出 / 导入**

您可将训练数据导出为加密文件（PBKDF2-HMAC-SHA256，100,000 次迭代）。导出的文件完全由您控制，本应用不会将导出数据上传至任何地方。

**5. 应用偏好设置**

语言选择、试次数、振动开关、计时器显示等偏好仅保存在本地，不上传至任何服务器。

## English Version

### 1. Information We Collect

**a) Training Data (stored locally on your device only)**

Your training records are stored locally on your device, including:

- Test mode (Color-Word / Animals / Plants)
- Session start and end times, total duration
- Per-trial stimulus content, display color, user response, and reaction time (ms)
- Cognitive analysis results (RT mean/median/SD, Stroop Interference Index SI, facilitation effect, Delta Score, IES, BIS, PES, etc.)

All training data is encrypted with **AES-256-CBC** before being stored. Encryption keys are managed by the operating system's secure storage (iOS/macOS: Keychain; Android: EncryptedSharedPreferences).

**b) iCloud Sync (Optional — iOS / macOS only)**

If you are signed in to iCloud, your training records may be synced to your personal iCloud account via Apple CloudKit. This data belongs solely to you — we have no access to it.

**c) Training Reminder Notifications (Optional)**

If you enable daily training reminders, the app registers local notifications on your device. Only your chosen reminder time and day-of-week preferences are stored locally. Notifications are triggered entirely on-device and are not connected to any remote service.

**d) Data Export / Import**

You may export your training data as an encrypted file (PBKDF2-HMAC-SHA256, 100,000 iterations). Exported files are entirely under your control — the app never uploads exported data anywhere.

**e) App Preferences**

Your language, trial count, vibration, and timer display preferences are saved locally only and are never uploaded to any server.

- We do not collect names, emails, phone numbers, or any personal identifiers

- We do not integrate any advertising SDK (no AdMob or similar services)
- We do not use any third-party analytics or crash-reporting services
- We never sell or share your data with third parties
- We cannot access your iCloud data

Training data exists only on your device and in your iCloud account. Uninstalling the app removes all local data. iCloud data can be deleted manually via iCloud settings. You may also delete all local training records at any time via **Settings → Danger Zone → Delete All Training Data** within the app.
